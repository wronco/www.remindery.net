Frequently Asked Questions

Will it work for my business model?

Remindery works with any kind of online business. Subscription services, infoproducts, physical retail sales, digital downloads, recurring payments, or even preorders.

What's the commitment?

No commitment. Remindery is a month-to-month subscription. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time.

I need to pay by check. Can you invoice me?

We can invoice large stores who subscribe to a yearly plan. Contact us at hello@remindery.net to arrange this. All monthly plans must be paid by credit card.
